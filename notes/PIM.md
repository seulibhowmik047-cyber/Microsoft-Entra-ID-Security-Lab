# Privileged Identity Management (PIM)

## What is PIM?

Microsoft Entra Privileged Identity Management (PIM) helps organizations manage, control, and monitor privileged access.

## Why is PIM Important?

PIM helps reduce excessive and permanent privileged access.

## Key Concepts

- Eligible Assignment
- Active Assignment
- Role Activation
- Justification
- Approval
- Time-Limited Access

## Just-In-Time Access

PIM allows privileged access to be activated only when required.

## Benefits

- Reduces standing privileged access
- Supports Least Privilege
- Provides better control over administrative roles
- Helps monitor privileged activities
